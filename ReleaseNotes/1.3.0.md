# CapDeck 1.3.0

## Copy text from your captures

- **Capture Text (⌃⇧T)** — drag over any text on screen and the recognized
  text lands on the clipboard, ready to paste. No image, no file, no preview.
- **Copy Text** is also available in the Preview window (⌘T), on every
  Capture History row, and in the Annotation editor.
- In the Annotation editor, crop first to copy only that region's text —
  and blurred areas stay out of the result.
- Recognition runs entirely on your Mac with Apple Vision. Nothing leaves
  the machine, and languages are detected automatically.

## A new Settings window

- Settings now uses a sidebar layout, making each section quicker to reach
  and leaving room for what comes next.
